TUPT-SSS
Laravel Vue jQuery

Technological University of the Philippines - Taguig Subject Scheduling System (TUPT-SSS) for our thesis is a system where a scheduler can drag and drop blocks of subjects with the assigned professor and room to a school calendar week for a particular class. The scheduler can then adjust the subject to a time or schedule which he/she prefers. The system's main purpose is to detect error, overlaps and anomalies in the schedule which will notify the scheduler as soon as it detects one. It also has its management part where the administrator can manage the subjects, rooms, professors, schedules etc. which is helpful for maintaining the records. And in addition to that we included a simple messaging system, recycling of schedule and more.
